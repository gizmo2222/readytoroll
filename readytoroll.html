ReadyToRoll Help

Getting Started
1. Set your state — go to Settings and pick your state. The app will show the correct total and night-hour requirements.

2. Enter your name — shown in Settings and on your printed log.

3. Set a goal date — optional target date for your road test. The app will show how many hours per week you need.
Logging Drives
🚗 Start Drive — tap on the Home tab. ReadyToRoll records your GPS route, weather, and time automatically. Allow location access for the best results — it's optional but enables route maps and weather.

+ Add Manually — enter a past drive by date, start/end time, and conditions. Use this for drives you forgot to log, or when GPS isn't available.

Tags — tap tag chips (Highway, Rain, Parking…) before saving to categorise each drive.

Supervisor — enter your supervisor's name and licence number in the manual entry form. This appears on your printed log.

Day vs Night — detected automatically from your local sunrise/sunset. You can override it in the manual entry form.
Progress Tab
The ring shows your overall % complete. The bars below break out night and day hours separately.

Goal Date — set a target test date in Settings. ReadyToRoll will show how many hours per week you need to stay on track.

Milestones — badges unlock automatically as you hit thresholds (first drive, 25%, halfway, etc.). Confetti fires when a new one is earned.

Weekly Chart — shows the last 8 weeks of activity, split by day (amber) and night (purple).
Skills Checklist
26 skills across 6 categories mirror a standard DMV road test. Tap a skill name to expand tips. Mark skills Learning or Mastered — tap the active button again to deselect it.

The number badge on the Skills tab shows how many skills haven't been started yet.
History
All drives listed by month. Use the filter chips to show only day or night drives. Search by location, supervisor, or tag. Tap any drive to see the full detail, route map, and notes.

Edit — tap a drive → Edit to correct time, conditions, or supervisor.

Delete — tap a drive → Delete. A 5-second Undo option appears in the notification bar.
Settings
State — changes the hour requirement. You can override the total and night hours if your instructor or parent has different targets.

Dark mode — toggle under Appearance.

Accent colour — 6 colour options.

Text size — Small / Medium / Large.
Data & Export
Export JSON — full backup of all drives and settings. Use Import JSON to restore.

Export CSV — open in Excel or Google Sheets for custom reporting.

Print Log — generates a formatted log with session table, supervisor column, skills checklist, and signature lines — ready to hand to a DMV officer.

Tip: export a JSON backup before clearing data or switching devices.
Phone Lock & Background
ReadyToRoll keeps your screen awake during a drive (wake lock). If you manually lock the phone the GPS route will pause — the total drive time will still be accurate because it's calculated from start/stop timestamps, not a timer. Distance and route map will have gaps. For a complete route, keep the screen on and the app in the foreground.
